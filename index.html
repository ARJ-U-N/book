Bill Cipher

Bill Cipher is a two-dimensional interdimensional demon from the now-destroyed dimension, Euclydia, formerly existent only in the Mindscape before succeeding in gaining access to the real world. He had been running amok in Gravity Falls, Oregon since being summoned by Ford Pines over thirty years ago. Known for his mysterious demeanor and sadistic humor, Bill is the main antagonist of the overall series.

Mabel Pines

Mabel is an energetic 12-year-old girl (she and her brother turn 13 at the end of the series finale) who spends her summer vacation at her great-uncle's tourist trap called the "Mystery Shack", located in the fictional town of Gravity Falls, Oregon.

Wendy Corduroy

Wendy is a tall, laid-back teenager working part-time at the Mystery Shack. Fun-loving and rebellious, she frequently shirks responsibility for more enjoyable activities like hanging out with her friends or going on adventures with Dipper and Mabel Pines.
